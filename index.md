## Be on a lookout for my blog!

Ok most of my site is still under construction. If you've checked it out before, you would have noticed the flashy text and animation trics. I can't wait to write the first blog post. I'll be using [forestry.io](https://forestry.io) as my cms (great customer service by the way) and I'll continue adding content to my site. Also, I'm missing a dedicated domain name so I'll add that too.
